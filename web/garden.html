Field of Dreams ~ Our Vegetable Garden

The garden, source of inspiration & nurishment:

Created early in 2008 the garden has literaly sprung up out of nowhere. By using irrigation techniques that are thousands of years old the fertile land that was under utilised at the rear of the farm has been transformed into a blossoming garden of Eden.

Its main purpose is two fold; to educate the children in farming techniques and obviously the results will benefit the cooking pot as well. Surplus remainders of the crops are distributed to the undernourished local community.

Current List of what we grow:

  • Spring Onions
  • Normal Onions
  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Pumpkins
  • Lettuce
  • Chilies
  • Leeks
  • Spinach
  • More planned...

The first crops are harvested

The first batch of spinach was harvested on Sunday 6th April 2008. Many of the other vegetables are close to being ready and everyone is looking forward to eating the vegetables they have helped to plant and grow. It is also wonderful to witness the sense of pride that the children feel and show knowing that they helped create the food they are about to eat.
