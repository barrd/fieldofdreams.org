Field of Dreams ~ About Funding & Sponsors

Our Very Generous Yearly Sponsors:

We would like to thank one and all who have contributed, below are some of the people that have helped over the last few years:

Earthwalk

Located in Homer, Alaska they have helped with the treatment of the Esperado and local community children. Offering structual integaration, counselling of care givers and children alike, they have also introduced Homeopathy, Aromatherapy, Bach Flower Essences and Reflexology.

Pick n' Pay

South African Super Market chain who supply food three times a week to the farm and has done so for more than 12 years.

NG Church Nelspruit

A family cell group from Nelspruit, Mpumalanga who supply us with second hand furniture, sundries and food.

Seed of Light

A United States group of individuals who supply a quarterly budget to help facilitate running costs.

Friends of Esperado (Jersey, UK)

A British organisation who help supply funds for specific projects and needs.
