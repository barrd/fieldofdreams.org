A short introduction to Field of Dreams:

Field of Dreams was created when Sophia Jardim put her dream into reality; to create a different environment from the orphanages and care centres for children from surrounding poverty stricken areas. Field of Dreams is a space where children can experience what life is about; love, care, and being heard as a child. Please select a link below for more information, or select another link from the right hand menu. Read our brief history or the Field of Dreams achievements page.

We need your help & support:

Funding is always a problem. With very little Government financial support, we are constantly struggling to make ends meet. Only six children within our care are covered by any form of Governmental fiscal aid and this is obviously very inadequate. If you are capable or willing to lend support in any form then please contact us by eMail. We look forward to hearing from you.

Important areas that we feel need to be met:

The bullet points below are a brief outline of where our objectives lie, within this site are more pages to explain many in more detail.

Our Focus Points:

  • That their voices must be heard
  • Create a space where they feel safe
  • A loving environment during school holidays
  • Experience and respect of other cultures
  • Being in touch with nature
  • Team spirit and team building
  • Belief in themselves (self confidence)

Our Key Objectives:

  • To make the world a better place
  • To give children a chance in life
  • To let mankind respect each others cultures
  • To let children have a childhood
  • Enable the children to have an education
