Introduction to the Volunteers

Over the last few years there have been several International volunteers that have helped out at Field of Dreams. They have come from far and wide and all have taken away a piece of Africa with them as it is never just a one way process.

About Funding / Sponsorship:

Continue to Funding

We need help with General day to day running costs. To this end we already have several organisations who contribute yearly to specific projects.

NG Church Nelspruit

A family cell group from Nelspruit who supply us with second hand furniture, sundries and food.

Seed of Light

A United States group of individuals who supply a quarterly budget to help facilitate basic running costs.

Friends of Esperado (Jersey)

A British organisation who help supply funds for specific projects and needs.

Pick n' Pay

South African Super Market chain who supply food three times a week to the farm and has done so for more than 12 years.
